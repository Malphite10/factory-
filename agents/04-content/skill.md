# Content Strategy Skill

## Required Output
- **Copy Inventory**: Headlines, taglines, body copy, CTA labels.
- **SEO Optimization**: Primary keywords, secondary keywords, meta titles (60 chars), meta descriptions (160 chars).
- **Tone of Voice**: Brand-aligned adjectives, voice guidelines, common phrases to avoid.

## Rules
- Use high-converting action verbs for CTAs.
- Ensure all copy is accessible (Screen reader friendly).
- Maintain 8th-grade reading level for accessibility.

## Validation
- Copy matches information architecture in product-spec.
- Keywords are integrated naturally.
